Quick and easy picked onions that go on pretty much anything.
### Ingredients
- 2-3 Large Onions
- 1 cup of White Vinegar
- 1 cup of Water
- 2 tbsp of Peppercorns
- 3 Bay Leaves
- 1 tbsp of Chili Flakes
- 1 tbsp of Mustard Seeds
- 1 tbsp of Kosher Salt
- 2 tsp of Sugar

### Instructions
1. Half the onions and slice them very thin
2. Shove them all into a mason jar until packed tight
3. Combine the rest of the ingredients together in a pot and bring to a boil
4. Once at a slight boil, take off the heat and pour into the mason jar leaving no air
5. Let sit for at least 30 min (or until cool)

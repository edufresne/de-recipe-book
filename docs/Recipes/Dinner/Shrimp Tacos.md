### Ingredients
- 1 Package of Frozen Deveined Deshelled Shrimp
- 1 Bundle of Cilantro
- 2 Cups of Salsa (either Taqueira or Salsa Verde)
- 1 Avocado
- Corn Tortillas
- 1 tbsp Cumin
- 1 tbsp Paprika
- 2 tbsp Olive Oil
- Salt & Pepper
- 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
- 1 tbsp Onion Powder
- A good amount of sunflower oil

### Instructions
1. Defrost and wash the shrimp and pat them dry
2. In a bowl season the shrimp with olive oil and all the spices and set aside
3. Fry the tortillas until they are cooked in the sunflower oil on a hot cast iron (similar to a comal)
4. Slice up the avocado and cilantro
5. Fry the shrimp in oil for about 3 minutes until they have changed color
6. Pour the salsa and cook them in the salsa for a minute or two while stirring frequently
7. Cut up the shrimp into smaller pieces and place them on the tacos
8. Top with the avocado, [Mexican Crema](../../Prep/Mexican%20Crema.md), [Picked Onions](../../Prep/Pickled%20Onions.md) and the cilantro
